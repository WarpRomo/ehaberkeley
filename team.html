At the Environmental Health Alliance at UC Berkeley, we are a passionate and dedicated group of students, researchers, and faculty committed to making a difference. Our team brings diverse expertise and a shared vision for a healthier, more sustainable world. We are here to support, inspire, and work alongside you in our collective efforts to protect the environment. Whether you're a student, professional, or community member, we invite you to join us in our mission. Together, we can create meaningful change and drive the innovations needed for a sustainable future. Come be a part of our vibrant community and help us make a lasting impact!
